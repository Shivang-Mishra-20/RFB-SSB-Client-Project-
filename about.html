About RFB SSB

Premier coaching institute for SSB Interview with a proven track record of success

RFB SSB is a premier coaching institute dedicated to guiding aspirants towards success in the Indian Armed Forces. With a team of experienced faculty and a commitment to excellence, we provide top-notch coaching for SSB Interview.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in Defence coaching by shaping confident, capable officers through expert training and mentorship. We offer affordable classes to ensure every aspirant has access to quality guidance and the opportunity to serve the nation.

Our Mission

We aim to empower candidates with knowledge, discipline, and confidence. Our coaching develops leadership skills, mental agility, and physical fitness - crucial for success in the Defence sector.

Our Success

With over 80+ successful recommendations out of 400 enrollments in various Defence academies, we take pride in our candidates' achievements and their contributions to the nation.

Our Faculty

Our faculty includes subject experts and Defence selection specialists with deep knowledge of the SSB process. They provide personalized coaching, practical strategies, and scientific assessment techniques to help candidates develop their leadership potential and officer-like qualities.

Our Methodology

Our holistic approach combines academic rigor with personality development to create well-rounded candidates:

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Structured program covering all aspects of SSB Interview
  • Personalized Attention: Small batch sizes with individual mentoring
  • Practical Training: Regular drills, group discussions, and mock interviews
  • Performance Analytics: Detailed feedback and progress tracking

Our Facility

Visit our state-of-the-art training center equipped with:

  • Theory with line by line assessment of psych response.
  • Regular GD & GPE practice and rigorous brainstorming on the structures related to GTO tasks.
  • Theory and one-to-one PIQ analysis with comprehensive mock interview
